Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the internal social media publishing tool operated by Intelligent Design Works Limited ("IDW3D", "we", "us"). This tool is used exclusively by IDW3D for posting content to our own social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.

Who This Applies To

This tool is for personal/internal use only. It is not a public-facing application and is not available to third parties. No end-user accounts are created, and no personal data from third parties is collected or processed.

Data We Access

Through integration with the TikTok for Developers API, we access the following data scopes for our own TikTok account only:

• user.info.basic — to verify the connected account
• video.upload — to upload video content to our own account
• video.publish — to publish video content to our own account

How We Use This Data

Access tokens are stored locally on our own server and are used only to post content created by IDW3D to our own social media profiles. We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time through the respective platform's developer settings. No user data is retained beyond what is required for the active session.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@idw3d.com.
